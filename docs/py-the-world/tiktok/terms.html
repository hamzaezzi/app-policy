Terms of Service for Py-The-World - TikTok Video Publisher

Py-The-World - TikTok Video Publisher is an application that publishes video content to a TikTok account using the TikTok API.

The application is used to upload and publish videos generated by the system.

The application does not interact with end users and does not provide a public service.

By authorizing Py-The-World - TikTok Video Publisher, the TikTok account owner grants permission for the application to publish video content on their behalf.

The account owner remains responsible for the content published through the application.
